About

Founded in 2001, Namecheap is a domain registration and hosting company, that just so happens to be on the cutting edge of the internet.

Back when the internet was hating on the SOPA in the United States, competitor GoDaddy stood out in support of the bill - and Namecheap stepped up and expressed their disapproval of the bill, causing many domain registrants to transfer from GoDaddy to Namecheap.

Namecheap has since started accepting Bitcoin, and has proved to be a great source to purchase domain names, which (surprise!) is who DailyBitociner registered their name with.
